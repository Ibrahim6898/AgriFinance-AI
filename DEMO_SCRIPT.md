# AgriFinance AI - 3-Minute Demo Script

## Welcome & The Problem (0:00 - 0:45)
- **Visual:** Presenter in the bottom right, screen showing the AgriFinance AI landing page with farmers working in a field.
- **Script:** "Welcome to AgriFinance AI. Across Sub-Saharan Africa, millions of smallholder farmers are stuck in a poverty trap. They can't get micro-loans to buy high-yield seeds because banks require credit history—a history these farmers don't have. Furthermore, with unpredictable climate change, banks view farming operations as 'too risky' without data."
- **Action:** Transition to evaluating the actual platform.

## The Solution: AI Credit Scoring & User Onboarding (0:45 - 1:45)
- **Visual:** Navigate to `/onboard`. Begin filling out the form for "Amina Bello", entering 3.5 acres of sorghum, 8 years of experience, and a lack of prior loans.
- **Script:** "AgriFinance AI solves this by translating alternative data into a trustworthy credit profile. Here we have a farmer, Amina. She has 8 years of experience but no banking history. Our mobile-first platform lets NGOs or Amina herself input basic farm metrics."
- **Action:** Click "Generate Credit Score". Show the "Analyzing Farm Data..." loading state.

## Results & Green Tips (1:45 - 2:30)
- **Visual:** The ScoreCard appears (Score: 85, Grade: A). Highlight the "Climate Risk Level," "Our Analysis" (Gemini output), and the "Climate-Smart Green Tips".
- **Script:** "Our Google Gemini AI engine analyzes this data. You can see it evaluates Amina as an 'A' grade borrower. It clearly lists positive factors and risk factors in plain English. Most importantly, it calculates a Climate Risk Level and delivers actionable 'Green Tips' tailored to Sorghum farming in her region. This mitigates the lender's risk while educating the farmer on resilient practices."

## Digital Inclusion & Admin Dashboard (2:30 - 3:30)
- **Visual:** Scroll down to the "Simulate USSD / SMS Flow" component. Toggle between the USSD mockup and the SMS text mockup. Then quickly jump to the `/admin` portal showing the table of farmers.
- **Script:** "We understand that many of our primary users operate on 2G networks without smartphones. That's why the platform includes SMS and USSD integrations, delivering the AI's simplified analysis straight to feature phones in local languages. 
Finally, Microfinance institutions use our aggregated dashboard to proactively find and match the safest farming investments. AgriFinance AI: Empowering farmers, protecting the environment, and pioneering true digital and financial inclusion."
- **Action:** Display the "AgriFinance AI" logo and team contact info on the final slide.
